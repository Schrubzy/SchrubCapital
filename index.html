Investment Philosophy

Value-Oriented

Focus on companies trading below intrinsic value. The companies I analyse tend to fall into three broad categories: (1) overlooked compounders trading at discounts due to challenging economics or prevailing negative sentiment (2) event-driven or catalyst-based opportunities, and (3) asset-backed or cash-flow harvesting plays"

Long-Term Horizon

Patient capital approach with 3-5 year average investment horizons, allowing for compound value creation.

Fundamental Analysis

We focus on fundamental analysis: dissecting unit-economics, industry dynamics, and really understanding all drivers to a business model. Unlike large pods or funds, we can't compete on speed or infrastructure. We believe that with temperament, patience, and first-principles thinking, we can hold differentiated views that will outperform in the long-run.
